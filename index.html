Explore any toll booth
Every diamond marker on the map is a toll station. Hover over any marker to see prices for motorcycles, cars and vans. Click any marker to open a detailed panel showing:
  • Prices for all 4 vehicle categories
  • Which direction(s) you pay
  • Bypass options — the exact exit and entry ramps to use, with the extra time per direction
  • The bypass route drawn in green on the map
Click anywhere on the map to close the panel.
⊕
Analyse a route
Use the bar at the bottom for personalised advice on any journey:
  1. Enter your origin and destination
  2. Select your vehicle type
  3. Set your time value — how many minutes of extra driving you'd accept to save €1. At 10 min/€1 your time is worth €6/hr.
  4. Press Analyse
The app draws your route, finds every toll on it, and tells you which are worth paying and which to bypass. Green = avoid, blue = pay, yellow = marginal.
💡 Tip: Bypass routes are based on the 2026 Greek toll avoidance guide. Always verify with Google Maps before taking an unfamiliar detour.
